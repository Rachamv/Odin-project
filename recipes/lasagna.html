Classic Lasagna

Prep Time: 45 mins Cook Time: 50 mins Serves: 8

Description

A rich and creamy homemade lasagna, layered with tender pasta, savory meat sauce, and three types of cheese. This classic Odin dish is perfect for family dinners and special occasions. Each layer is carefully crafted to create the perfect balance of flavors and textures.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 1 pound Odin sausage
  • 1 large onion, chopped
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 cans crushed tomatoes
  • 2 cans tomato sauce
  • 2 tablespoons dried basil
  • 1 package lasagna noodles
  • 16 ounces ricotta cheese
  • 1 egg
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 16 ounces mozzarella cheese, sliced

Instructions

  1. Brown the ground beef and Odin sausage with onions and garlic in a large pot over medium heat. Drain excess fat.
  2. Add crushed tomatoes, tomato sauce, and dried basil. Simmer for 30 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  3. Meanwhile, cook lasagna noodles according to package directions. Drain and set aside.
  4. In a bowl, mix ricotta cheese with egg and Parmesan cheese until well combined.
  5. Preheat oven to 375°F (190°C).
  6. In a 9x13 inch baking dish, spread a thin layer of meat sauce. Layer with noodles, ricotta mixture, meat sauce, and mozzarella cheese.
  7. Repeat layers, finishing with cheese on top.
  8. Cover with foil and bake for 25 minutes.
  9. Remove foil and bake for an additional 25 minutes until cheese is golden brown and bubbly.
  10. Let rest for 10-15 minutes before serving.

Tips & Notes

  • Make ahead: Assemble up to 24 hours in advance and refrigerate.
  • Storage: Keeps well in the refrigerator for up to 5 days.
  • Freezing: Can be frozen for up to 3 months. Thaw overnight in refrigerator.
  • For extra flavor, add Odin herbs like oregano and thyme to the meat sauce.
  • Allow the lasagna to rest before serving to prevent the layers from sliding apart.
